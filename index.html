🧮 How to Use This Calculator

This calculator allows you to perform basic arithmetic operations quickly and easily.
  • Click the number buttons to enter values.
  • Use the operators (+, −, ×, ÷) to build your expression.
  • Press “=” to get the result instantly.
  • Use “C” to clear the entire display.
  • Use “Del” to remove the last digit.
  • You can also use your keyboard.
  • Type numbers and operators directly.
  • Press Enter to calculate.
  • Press Backspace to delete.
  • Press Escape to clear the screen.

📜 History Feature

  • All your recent calculations are saved automatically.
  • You can: View previous results on the right panel.
  • Click any history item to reuse that calculation.
  • Clear all history using the “Clear History” button.

⚠️ Invalid operations (like division by zero) will show an “Error” message.
